LATIN FOR THE END OF THE WORLD

Vocabulary Flash Cards

There is no need to feel any compulsion to work through this entire list of vocabulary from Lessons XIII through XXIII. After all, there are more than 200 words here, presented in random order.

This volume does not contain many pairs or sets of words with overlapping meanings. However, note that we have a noun for happiness and a word for joy, which are only partially synonymous. From the perspective of the ancient world, and of ancient Romans in particular, joy is a momentary, transitory emotion, whereas happiness is more of a long-term condition.
