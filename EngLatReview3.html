LATIN FOR THE END OF THE WORLD

Vocabulary Flash Cards

Volume III introduces nearly 200 new vocabulary words and reintroduces several others. It focuses mostly on nouns and adjectives, but it does include a few verbs and others. As you cycle through this set of English-to-Latin flash cards:

  • For each noun, remember the Nominative and Genitive singular forms, and whether they have an -ium in the Genitive plural.
  • For each adjective, remember the Nominative singular forms to agree masculine, feminine, and neuter nouns. (We may be using uter to refer to persons who can be either masculine or feminine, but as far as we know, the Romans did not introduce an uter column in their declension tables.)

As usual, for each word, you can wait five seconds to see whether what you've remembered is correct.
