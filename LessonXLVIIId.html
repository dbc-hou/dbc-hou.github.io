Grammar and Syntax—Verbs

Synopses: Active Voice Only

Rather than have you conjugate verbs completely in all six tenses, for each column below write a synopsis, a cross-section of the conjugation: the forms of the six tenses for a single subject (person-number conjugation), plus the perfect active infinitive (to have verbed) for each verb.

There is no need to include the passive-voice forms; however, you can practice in your head converting active to passive in the present system.

This is somewhat repetitious, but in a way that will build your confidence in conjugating.
