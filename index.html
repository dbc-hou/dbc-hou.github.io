LATIN FOR THE END OF THE WORLD

Author: David B. Collins

David has worked in the field of Information Technology since 1996, in a variety of roles, but mostly as a training specialist. Before landing in the IT world, he taught multiple subjects in secondary schools in Connecticut and Texas, specializing in Latin.

This interactive Latin language learning site is David's revival of a project that he began in late 2002 and shelved soon afterward. It combines some moderately sophisticated HTML, JavaScript/JQuery, and Cascading StyleSheets (CSS); eventually it will include components built with Java and a MySQL database.

David's Texas-based novels A Small Town for Its Size and The Earthworm That Blows No Trumpet are available via iUniverse.com. You can also find links to them at dbcgreentx.net, which features his free online novel Eastern Daylight.
