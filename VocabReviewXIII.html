LATIN FOR THE END OF THE WORLD

Vocabulary—Lessons XXXVII-XXXVIII

Let's take in a quick review and see how well you know time.

The vocabulary in this review comes only from Lessons XXXVII and XXXVIII, all having to do with time— including the ordinal adjectives through twelfth and the months of the year (which should not be very difficult) There is a mix of nouns, adjectives, and adverbs. Again, you can choose Latin to English translation or vice versa.

Remember that the names of the months are adjectives: seven are of the -us/-a/-um variety, and the rest are Declension III.
